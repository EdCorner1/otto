# Otto Blog — 30-Day Content Calendar
**Audience:** New UGC creators (zero to early-stage) who need clients, clarity, and momentum.
**Tone:** Opinionated field manual — B + C from the style options. Direct, no fluff, real numbers.
**Proof points:** Ed's 0→$6K/month, 5 secured deals, 4-month timeline — used authentically, not humble-brag.

---

## Content Pillars
1. **UGC Foundations** — how to start, land first client, avoid beginner traps
2. **Client Economics** — rates, negotiation, what's worth doing vs. noise
3. **Brand Playbook** — finding clients, pitching, building relationships that pay
4. **Creator Growth** — audience that attracts brands (not the other way around)
5. **Tool Stack** — honest takes on apps and tools worth your time
6. **Win Stories** — case studies, what actually worked (including failures)
