# Otto Roadmap

_Last updated: 2026-04-22_

## Product framing
Otto is not just a marketplace.
It is becoming:
1. a creator onboarding funnel,
2. a public creator portfolio website,
3. a brand/creator workflow layer,
4. and eventually a monetized creator operating system.

---

## Stage 1 — MVP
**Goal:** get creators live fast with credible public profiles brands can actually review.

### Must-haves
- Creator onboarding that reliably completes
- Required 3-video minimum for creator quality
- Portfolio video upload that feels smooth
- Category-based portfolio tabs with newest-first ordering
- Public creator profile preview that looks polished and shareable
- Basic creator discovery / browse flow for brands
- Clean live campaign / deal status visibility

### Homepage / landing page layer
- Build-in-public roadmap section styled like a social feed
- Simple thumbs up / thumbs down reactions on roadmap cards
- "What do you want to see?" feedback capture section
- Social-friendly, screenshot-friendly design
- Clear creator/brand value props

### Success criteria
- A creator can onboard without confusion
- A creator ends with 3+ videos and a profile that feels real
- A brand can evaluate creator quality quickly
- The landing page creates trust, momentum, and audience feedback

### Not now
- Full no-code page builder
- Custom domains
- Scheduling integrations
- Complex monetization logic
- Public commenting/community system

---

## Stage 2 — Traction / Product-Market Fit
**Goal:** improve creator quality, brand confidence, and workflow stickiness.

### Focus areas
- Better creator profile templates / public portfolio site system
- `name.ottougc.com` public creator sites
- More robust creator trust signals and proof points
- Better search / filtering for brands
- Stronger admin review and curation flows
- Better brief creation and brand onboarding
- Workflow improvements around creator-brand collaborations

### Success criteria
- More creators finish onboarding and publish profiles
- More brands meaningfully browse / contact / shortlist
- More creator profiles are shareable and conversion-ready

---

## Stage 3 — Monetization / Scale
**Goal:** convert Otto from a useful platform into a durable business.

### Focus areas
- Paid brand plans
- Featured placements / boosts
- Creator Pro plan
- Custom domains for Pro creators
- Advanced analytics
- Scheduling / creator operations layer
- Metricool or equivalent scheduling integration
- Brand/creator execution workflow tied to deliverables and publishing

### Pricing direction (current thinking)
#### Brands
- Browse free
- Starter: $149/mo
- Growth: $349/mo
- Scale: $699/mo

#### Creators
- Free to join
- 10% platform fee later
- Creator Pro: $29/mo later
- Creator fees waived in earliest phase to grow supply side

### Success criteria
- Pricing feels earned, not premature
- Paid features clearly map to workflow value
- Creators see Otto as a business tool, not just a profile page

---

4. Homepage roadmap/feed + feedback layer
5. Subdomain creator-site system planning
